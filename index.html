ABOUT

Pretty Parlor Cloud M, where style meets serenity. Originally from Pella, Northern Cape, the business has opened up a new salon in Pofadder. The salon is more than just a place for hair transformations—it’s a haven for self-care and rejuvenation. As a passionate team of hair artisans, the company believes that every strand tells a story.
Mission Statement: At Pretty Parlor Cloud M, the mission is to empower clients through personalized hair experiences. The company is not just about cuts and colours but is also about confidence and connection. Their commitment is to listen, create, and elevate. Whether a client seeks a bold new look or a subtle enhancement, Pretty Parlor Cloud M is dedicated to making all their customers feel beautiful from the inside out.
Vision Statement: The vision is to be the go-to destination for hair transformations in the Khai-Ma region. Pretty Parlor Cloud M aims to be the bridge between timeless elegance and contemporary flair. The company envisions a space where laughter echoes, mirrors reflect dreams, and scissors dance with precision. As the business grows, it aspires to collaborate with local artists, photographers, and musicians, creating a vibrant community that celebrates beauty in all its forms.
